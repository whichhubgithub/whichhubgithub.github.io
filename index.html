Name Origin

The surname 'Düzelli' has Turkish origins

• 'Düz' means 'straight' or 'plain' in Turkish
• 'el' means 'hand' or metaphorically 'skill/mastery' in Turkish
• '-li' suffix indicates 'from' or 'of'
