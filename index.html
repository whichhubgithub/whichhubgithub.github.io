How to Pronounce "Düzelli":

Düzelli is pronounced approximately as "doo-ZEL-lee".

Dü: The "ü" is not common in English. Think of it as "doo" with your lips rounded, similar to the vowel sound in "food."

zel: Sounds like "zel" in "Zelda" (with a short "e" as in "bell").

li: Pronounced like "lee," as in the name "Lee."
